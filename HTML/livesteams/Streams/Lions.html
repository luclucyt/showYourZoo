Lions:

The lion, (Panthera leo), large, powerfully built cat (family Felidae) that is second in size only to the tiger.
The lion is a well-muscled cat with a long body, large head, and short legs. Size and appearance vary considerably between the sexes.
The proverbial “king of beasts,” the lion has been one of the best-known wild animals since earliest times.
