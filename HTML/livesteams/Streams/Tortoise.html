Tortoise:

Tortoises are terrestrial reptiles that are part of the turtle family.
Unlike the other members of the turtle family, tortoises cannot swim! These land dwelling creatures have tough claws for digging instead of the webbed feet of other turtles.
A tortoise is a slow-moving animal with a shell into which it can pull its head and legs for protection. Tortoises eat leaves, grasses, fungi, and vegetables for most of their meals
