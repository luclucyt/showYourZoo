Penguins:

Penguins are seabirds in the family Spheniscidae. All penguins have a white belly and a dark, mostly black, back.
This is a type of camouflage to keep them safe when they swim because it makes them blend in with their background.
They use their wings to swim underwater, but they cannot fly in the air. They eat fish and other seafood. Penguins lay their eggs and raise their babies on land.
