Kangaroo:

All kangaroos have short hair, powerful hind legs, small forelimbs, big feet and a long tail.
They have excellent hearing and keen eyesight. Depending on the species, their fur coat can be red, grey or light to dark brown.
Their muscular tail is used for balance when hopping, and as another limb when moving about.
Thanks to their large feet, kangaroos can leap some 30 feet (9 meters) in a single bound, and travel more than 30 miles (48 kilometers) per hour.
