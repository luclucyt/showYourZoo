Rhinoceros:

A rhinoceros (rhino for short) is any animal in the family Rhinocerotidae. There are five living species.
Two of these species are native to Africa. Three of these species are also native to Southern parts of Asia.
The rhinoceros is a herbivore.
Its favourite food is plants, branches and bushes. Rhinoceroses have a large horn on the nose.
Their horns are not like those of other horned mammals: the rhinoceros' horn is made of keratin packed together very tightly.
